My Story

Making the first step

This journey started when I was a little boy curious about computers and technology. When I was studying secondary school, my mom took me to a software academy center, where I ended up spending all my three precious months of the summer time there. I was in an introduction to computer and office software course. The course caught my interest. From time to time, I became more interested in the new field ahead and amazed at how this whole thing operated on those computers. After the summer break, I eventually had three certificates in office software, basic image editing on Photoshop, and basic web designing. What a significant achievement for a 13-year-olds boy =)).
